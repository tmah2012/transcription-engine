# Meeting Summary Templates

Select based on meeting type detected in Step 3 of SKILL.md workflow.

---

## Demo / Presentation

Use when: vendor demos, product walkthroughs, technical evaluations, RFP presentations.

```markdown
## [Client/Prospect] — [Topic] | [Date]

### Meeting Overview
[2-3 sentences: attendees, format, purpose. Note audio/tech issues if relevant.]

**Virtual:** [names, titles, companies]
**In-room:** [names, titles, companies]

### [Presenting Company] — Key Capabilities Demonstrated
[Group by topic area. Sub-headers. Focus on what was SHOWN, not marketing claims.]

#### [Topic Area 1]
- [Capability with specifics]
- [Config shown, parameters discussed]

#### [Topic Area 2]
- [...]

### [Second Presenter, if applicable]
[Same structure]

### Client-Specific Requirements Discussed
- **[Requirement]:** [How addressed or deferred]
- **[Requirement]:** [...]

### Key Questions & Answers
- **Q: [Question as asked]** — A: [Answer, who answered]
- **Q: [...]** — A: [...]

### Open Items & Follow-ups
[Group commitments — not personal tasks]
- [ ] [Who] owes [what] — [context/deadline if mentioned]

### Observations for Savana Executive Team
[Analytical synthesis — not a list. Address:]
- How was Savana positioned?
- What resonated with the prospect?
- Competitive concerns or gaps?
- Messaging effectiveness?
- What to watch for in next sessions?

### Next Steps
- [Scheduled follow-ups with dates]
```

## Discovery / Sales

Use when: initial meetings, requirements gathering, pipeline qualification.

```markdown
## [Company] — [Meeting Type] | [Date]

### Meeting Overview
[2-3 sentences: who, why, outcome tone]

**Attendees:** [names, titles, companies]

### Prospect Context
- **Current state:** [Systems/processes today]
- **Pain points:** [What's driving the conversation]
- **Strategic goals:** [Where they want to go]
- **Timeline:** [Decision timeline, budget cycle, urgency]
- **Decision makers:** [Authority, influencers]

### Key Discussion Points
[By topic, not chronologically. What was said AND the implication.]

#### [Topic 1]
[Summary + deal implications]

### Competitive Landscape
- [Other vendors mentioned, positioning]

### Qualification Signals
- **Budget:** [Discussed or inferred]
- **Authority:** [Who decides, who blocks]
- **Need:** [How acute, how defined]
- **Timeline:** [Hard deadlines, soft targets]

### Open Items & Follow-ups
- [ ] [Who] owes [what]

### Deal Assessment
[2-3 sentence synthesis: where it stands, risk, opportunity]

### Next Steps
- [...]
```

## Internal / Strategy

Use when: team meetings, deal reviews, planning, internal syncs.

```markdown
## [Topic] — Internal | [Date]

### Attendees
[names, roles]

### Context
[1-2 sentences: why this meeting happened]

### Key Decisions Made
- [Decision and rationale]

### Discussion Summary
[By topic. Focus on conclusions, not deliberation.]

#### [Topic 1]
[Decided or left open]

### Action Items
- [ ] [Person] — [Task] — [Deadline if mentioned]

### Open Questions
- [Unresolved items needing further discussion]

### Next Meeting / Follow-up
- [...]
```

Use when: doesn't fit above categories.

```markdown
## [Meeting Title] | [Date]

### Overview

**Attendees:** [names, roles, companies]

### Key Discussion Points

#### [Topic 1]
[Summary]

### Questions Raised
- **Q:** [Question] — **A:** [Answer or "unresolved"]

### Action Items & Follow-ups
- [ ] [Who] — [What] — [When]

### Next Steps
- [...]
```
